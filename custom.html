Custom game

Welcome to Custom! here in custom, feel free to apply your own rules for any game you would like. Add your rules into the text box and press add after each rule. After you have written all of your rules, press the start button and your rules will show randomly. If you would like to not include a certain rule, click on the rule under Active Rules and it will be transferred to Non-active rules!
